Spend your holiday with us

Diamond villa is one of the most distinguished high-end luxury mansions on the Adriatic. This brand new vacation rental is situated on the amazing island of Korcula, the birth place of Marco Polo. This villa’s fabulous location offers complete solitude just a 3 minute drive from the town of Korcula and all it’s authentic gastronomic and cultural experiences.
